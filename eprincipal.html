ERP Software for Schools

Admissions Management

The admissions management module allows schools to manage the entire admissions process, from application submission to enrollment. It includes features such as online applications, applicant tracking, and automated decision-making.

Attendance Tracking

The attendance tracking module allows schools to keep track of student attendance in real-time. It includes features such as automated attendance taking, notifications for absent students, and reports on attendance patterns.

Gradebook Management

The gradebook management module allows teachers to manage student grades, assignments, and assessments. It includes features such as grade entry, grade calculations, and grade reports for students and parents.

Parent Portal

The parent portal module allows parents to access real-time information about their child's education. It includes features such as grade tracking, attendance reports, and communication with teachers.

Staff Management

The staff management module allows schools to manage staff information, such as payroll, benefits, and schedules. It includes features such as employee self-service, time and attendance tracking, and performance management.

Student Information System

The student information system module allows schools to manage student information, such as demographics, transcripts, and schedules. It includes features such as student self-service, academic advising, and course registration.
